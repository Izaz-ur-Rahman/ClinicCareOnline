Introduction

Dr. Ethan Reynolds, DDS is a Board Certified Licensed Dentist, specializing in providing complete, safe, gentle and effective dental treatment for a wide variety of conditions. With many years of dental training and education under his belt, he has treated hundreds of patients with neuromusculoskeletal conditions, arthritis, sports and auto related injuries. As a top dentist in Pasadena, Dr. Ethan has had stunning success with these conditions and many others.

Dr. Ethan joined forces at Flintridge Family dentist in 2012, and since then he has built "A Reputation for Excellence". With a holistic family wellness approach, he is a top Pasadena dentist and a specialist in areas of dental and Webster Technique for dental care.

Qualifications of Dr. Ethan Reynolds, DDS

Dr. Ethan is a highly skilled and compassionate dentist dedicated to providing exceptional dental care to patients of all ages. With a strong educational background and extensive clinical experience, Dr. Ethan offers comprehensive dental services to help patients achieve optimal oral health and beautiful smiles.

Education And Training:

  • Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from Dental School, where Dr. Ethan graduated with honors.
  • Completed rigorous coursework in various dental specialties, including restorative dentistry, preventive dentistry, oral surgery, and cosmetic dentistry.
  • Participated in hands-on clinical training, gaining proficiency in the latest dental techniques and technologies.

Licensing And Certification:

  • Licensed to practice dentistry by the State or Regional Licensing Board.
  • Holds certification in Basic Life Support (BLS) and Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS) to ensure patient safety and emergency preparedness.

Professional Memberships:

  • Active member of the American Dental Association (ADA), staying abreast of industry advancements and best practices.
  • Engaged member of the Academy of General Dentistry (AGD), dedicated to continuing education and professional development.
  • Affiliated with the [Local Dental Society or Association], fostering collaboration with peers and staying involved in the local dental community.

Clinical Expertise:

  • Proficient in a wide range of dental procedures, including dental cleanings, fillings, crowns, bridges, root canal therapy, extractions, and dental implants.
  • Skilled in cosmetic dentistry techniques such as teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, and dental bonding to enhance smiles and boost confidence.
  • Experienced in treating patients with dental anxiety and phobia, employing gentle techniques and a compassionate chairside manner to ensure comfort and relaxation.

Commitment To Excellence:

  • Committed to providing personalized care tailored to each patient's unique needs and goals.
  • Dedicated to staying current with the latest advancements in dentistry through ongoing education and training.
  • Passionate about building trusting relationships with patients based on integrity, transparency, and open communication.
